What do we do?

Cataract Surgery

Microincision cataract surgery (MICS) under topical Anaesthesia with all types of foldable IOLs done. Excellent cure rates by experienced surgeons and nurses with high tech preoperative workup with equipment like Optical Biometry, state of the art Phaco machines like Alcon Infinity, high end operating microscopes like Zeiss Lumera I and above all very dedicated and compassionate approach make our centre a preferred destination for many to undergo cataract surgery.

Glaucoma Care

We actively screen every patient entering our hospital with any complaints for the presence of glaucoma which left undiagnosed may affect the vision silently but profoundly. We have state of the art diagnostics like OCT, Octopus automated perimetry, applanation tonometry, Gonioscopy, Pachymetry etc. We offer medical treatment, laser treatment including the latest cyclo-G-6 micropulse laser system and surgical treatment for glaucoma and offer excellent lifelong follow-ups

Refractive Error

We assess the accurate refractive condition of our patients with manual and automated refraction. We diagnose myopia, hypermetropia, astigmatism, presbyopia and offer accurate treatment with spectacles or contact lenses. We focus on children's refractive status and look for the presence of any squint or lazy eye and start active interventions like glasses and patching. We have a state of the art optical outlet with scientific lens dispensing and a host of world-renowned lenses and frames with dedicated opticians offering excellent and service.

Retinal Surgery

We actively look for the presence of retinal problems in all our patients by dilated fundus examination. In this way, we can pick up abnormal retinal conditions like Diabetic Retinopathy at a relatively early stage and prevent visual loss. We have advanced diagnostics like OCT, Fundus camera, FFA, and state of the art surgical facilities. We offer medical treatment, retinal laser treatment, intravitreal injections, and offer vitreoretinal surgeries for conditions like vitreous haemorrhage, macular hole, retinal detachment with success.

Cornea and Refractive Surgery

We have advanced diagnostic equipment namely PENTACAM, which can detect corneal abnormalities like keratoconus at its earlier stages we offer preoperative workup for assessing the suitability for undergoing refractive surgeries like LASIK. For patients with higher refractive error and thin cornea, who are not suitable for LASIK, we offer Implantable Contact Lenses (ICL) surgery with good outcomes.

Oculoplastic Surgery

We have facilities to treat surgically abnormal lid conditions like ptosis, entropion, ectropion, tumours, orbital abnormalities like proptosis, lacrimal abnormalities like tear flow obstruction through our oculoplastic wing with excellent success rate. We offer excellent cure rates for pterygium with pterygium excision with cunjunctival autografts.
