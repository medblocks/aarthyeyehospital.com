Our Team

Dr Ramesh

MS, DO, DNB

Dr Ramesh did his MBBS and DO from Thanjavur Medical College. Further MS from Madurai Medical College. He obtained DNB from National Board, New Delhi. Trained in Microsurgery from Aravind in 1993. He trained in Phaco Surgery from Agarwal, Chennai in 2000. Then he trained in Retinal examination and lasers from Aravind in 2001. He served in Eye Department of Karur Government Hospital for 10 glorious years. He has done thousands of cataract surgeries to date.

Dr Uma Maheswari

MBBS, DO

Dr Uma did her MBBS from the prestigious Madras Medical College. Completed DO from Sri Ramachandra Medical College and Research Institute, Chennai. She then underwent training in Small Incision Cataract Surgery and Phaco Surgery. She has special interest in Pterygium excision with Conjunctival autografting. She has done thousands of eye surgeries to date. She's an excellent clinician and a skilful surgeon.

Dr Vishwa Nanda

MBBS, MS

She completed her MBBS from Chettinad Hospital and Research Institute, Chennai. She did her post-graduation in ophthalmology from Sri Manakula Vinayagar Medical College, Pondicherry. She has been awarded the gold medal for her outstanding performance in the master of surgery in ophthalmology examination by the Pondicherry institution. She has completed a 2-year fellowship in IOL and Anterior Segment from Aravind Eye Hospital, Coimbatore.

Dr G. Seethapathy

MS, MRCS, FRCSEd, FRCOphth (London)

Dual Subspecialty Fellowships-Orbital and Oculoplastic Surgery, Neuro-ophthalmology and Adult Ocular Motility. Senior Consultant Ophthalmic Plastic and Orbital Surgeon, Adjunct Professor in Ophthalmology, MAHE Dr Seethapathy is one of the few highly specialized Ophthalmic Plastic and Orbital Surgeons in Western Tamilnadu with an exclusive subspecialty practice of more than 12 years, encompassing the complete spectrum of Eyelid, Lacrimal and Orbital Surgery ranging from simple to complex. He has been a regular invited Faculty at local, National and International meetings.

Dr Brahadeesh Subramanian

MBBS, DO, DNB, FICO (Lon), FMRF

Dr Brahadeesh Subramanian is a consultant ophthalmologist and vitreoretinal surgeon.he has completed his ophthalmic education from Aravind eye hospital Madurai and Sankara Nethralaya Chennai. In his 10 years of practice, he has performed over 10000 eye surgeries including complicated cataract and retinal surgeries.

Dr Jayanthan Soundarapandian

MBBS, DO, DNB

Graduated from Tanjavur Medical College and finished his post-graduation of DO and DNB from the prestigious Aravind Eye Hospital and the Post Graduate Institute of Opthalmology, Madurai. Completed long term fellowship in paediatric ophthalmology and strabismus from the same institution. Had training in refractive surgery from Narayana Nethralaya, Bangalore. Has passed international council of ophthalmology exam. A surgeon with immense experience in cataract, cornea, squint and retina. Won best paper award in the field of cornea at TNOA 2015. He has a special interest in Keratoconus and pentacam analysis. Worked as a medical officer in Theni Eye Hospital. Is presently the chief consultant of paediatric ophthalmology and refractive surgery unit of KS Eye Hospital, Erode.
